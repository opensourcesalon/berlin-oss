SourceCred: Building an Open Source Economy

Dandelion Mane, Protocol Labs

Dandelion has led development on a few different open-source software projects: SourceCred, TensorBoard (component of TensorFlow), and Plottable.js. Right now their main focus is on SourceCred and SourceGrain, a pair of projects that aim to create a sustainable funding model for open-source. They are a software engineer at Protocol Labs, find them on Twitter @decentralion.

Panel: Open Source & Venture Investing

Moderator: Patrick Mayr, Cherry Ventures

Open source software has always underpinned the infrastructure of the Internet and other parts of the economy at large. Yet up until recently parts of the investor community looked at the open source business model with skepticism. The emergence and exits of large companies such as Red Hat, Mulesoft, MongoDB, Elastic, Github etc., have demonstrated not only the viability, but enormous potential of the industry. Crypto, a new asset class built entirely on open source software has received unprecedented sums of money from investors. We’ll be chatting to investors to get their take.
